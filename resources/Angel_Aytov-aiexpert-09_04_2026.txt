# AI Expert - Healthcare AI Systems

Company Overview:
AI Expert develops healthcare AI systems emphasizing privacy and compliance. They describe themselves as building "privacy-first, production-grade healthcare AI systems" following ISO/IEC 42001, HIPAA, and GDPR practices, though note they are not formally certified.

Core Services:
The company offers five main service categories:
- Platform Integration
- Privacy Engineering
- Clinical Documentation
- Decision Support
- Virtual Assistants

Primary Focus:
Their stated mission centers on systems "for healthcare operations, clinical workflows, and regulatory compliance." They position themselves as designing, deploying, and governing production-grade AI solutions tailored to organizational needs.
