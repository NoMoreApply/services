# Electacar - Electric Car Guide Hong Kong

Project Description:
Electacar is a comprehensive digital platform serving as a complete guide for electric vehicle (EV) shoppers in Hong Kong. The site enables users to "Compare EVs, check prices, find dealers, and learn about charging in Hong Kong."

Core Features:

Vehicle Information & Comparison:
- Database of electric vehicles available in Hong Kong with specifications including range, acceleration (0-100 km/h), power, and pricing
- Popular models featured include Tesla Model Y (719 km range), Tesla Model 3, BYD Sealion 7, and BYD ATTO 3
- Browse functionality organized by manufacturer, with brands including Audi, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, BYD, GAC Aion, Hyundai, Tesla, and Volvo

Resources & Tools:
- Dealer locator across Hong Kong with authorized service center information
- Educational guides covering EV ownership topics: charging infrastructure, government incentives, insurance, and maintenance
- EV tax calculator for first registration tax estimation
- Running cost comparison tool (EV vs. petrol vehicles)
- Insurance quote collection service partnering with brokers

Government Incentive Information:
The platform highlights Hong Kong's One-for-One EV Replacement Scheme, offering "Save up to HK$172,500" for qualifying vehicles, with a deadline of March 31, 2026.

Technical Architecture:
Built with Next.js framework using React components, featuring multiple theme options (default, electra, monochrome, editorial, ocean) with CSS variable-based theming system.
